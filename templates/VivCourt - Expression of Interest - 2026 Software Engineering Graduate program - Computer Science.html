      VivCourt

      Expression of Interest - 2026 Software Engineering Graduate program - Computer Science

      Share job ad

      AI Job Summary

      Read More

      WHO WE ARE

      Join VivCourt, where performance fuels purpose.


      We’re a trading firm that values excellence as much as impact. We bring the brightest minds together from various fields in STEM to trade diverse quantitative strategies, from systematic and positional trading to market-making.
      Our vision goes beyond profits. We prove that exceptional results and putting people first can go hand in hand. At VivCourt, your well-being matters, your curious mind and determination drives your growth, and your achievements go beyond to support meaningful causes.

      WHAT YOU’LL DO

      As a Software Engineering Graduate at VivCourt, you’ll quickly take on responsibility and make a meaningful impact. Collaborating with engineers and traders, you’ll design, build, and maintain high-performance trading systems that are critical to our success.
      You’ll tackle real-world challenges, improving the performance, reliability, and scalability of our trading systems. Supported by senior engineers and your team, you’ll receive comprehensive training to develop technical expertise and a pragmatic problem-solving approach. From day one, you’ll contribute to our trading platform and build the skills needed to spot opportunities that drive both your career and VivCourt forward.

      WHO YOU ARE


      Essential Skills:
      • A background in one of the following disciplines: Computer Science, Data Science, Electrical Engineering, Software Engineering, or any other quantitative discipline where coding is used
      • Strong knowledge of algorithms and data structures
      • Knowledgeable about object-oriented design and development concepts
      • A curious nature paired with a knack for solving complex problems
      • Be collaborative and pragmatic communicator who likes to get things done 
      • Experience with either C++, C#, GO or Python is required for our technical assessment

      Desirable Skills: 
      • Previous knowledge of financial markets through your own interest, an internship or a graduate program at another Trading firm. 
      • Thrives in a fast-paced environment, with the ability to effectively manage multiple priorities

      WHAT YOU’LL GET


      • Remuneration: A competitive salary and bonus package 
      • Flexibility: Hybrid work environment (3/2 split)
      • Learning & Development: In-class sessions with senior Engineers & Traders and a mentoring program
      • Relocation budget: Moving interstate? No worries. We cover flights and initial accommodation.
      • Giving back: You’ll get yearly impact day(s) and social bonuses that you can use to support a not-for-profit close to your heart. 

      In addition, you will enjoy free food and beverages, gym classes, in-house massages, a variety of other social activities, and an annual company trip. 

      HOW TO APPLY

      Click “Apply now” and submit your resume and academic transcript for the Expression of Interest - 2026 Software Engineering Graduate Program. Applications close on 31st March 2025. 
      We only accept students graduating in 2026, who are Australian or New Zealand citizens or Australian Permanent Residents. Find more information on our hiring process here.

      A SENSE OF BELONGING 


      We bring talented people together to do amazing work. Whoever you are, wherever you’re from, you’ll find a place to be your best self and do your best work at Vivcourt. We are a place where everyone can feel they belong and their unique value is celebrated. We strongly encourage applications from people from diverse cultures, genders, backgrounds, and abilities.
      We welcome candidates of all abilities and have designed our assessments and processes to be accessible to all. We encourage you to share any adjustments you may require to help you be successful through our process and support you in performing your best.
